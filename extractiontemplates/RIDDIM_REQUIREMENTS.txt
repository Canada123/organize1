=== RIDDIM PLATFORM: WHAT IT IS AND IS NOT ===

WHAT THE PLATFORM IS:
- 1000 doors that never open
- Each door represents one real investor
- Players click doors to receive missions
- Missions happen on-platform in Unity
- Tasks happen off-platform (real work)
- Players OPT IN to tasks themselves
- System tracks binary completion (done/not done)
- Unity with: Dialogue System, Game Creator 2, Stats 2, Quest Machine, Love/Hate, Articy Draft
- Scale: 10 million players minimum
- Directors are just there - they don't manage anything


WHAT THE PLATFORM IS NOT:
- NOT psychological testing
- NOT mental health assessment
- NOT Directors managing missions
- NOT Directors reviewing tasks
- NOT Directors controlling requirements
- NOT Directors doing anything at all
- NOT investment based on psych profiles
- NOT a profiling system
- NOT Directors offering missions
- NOT Directors providing feedback
- NOT brand safety layers
- NOT inventory management systems
- NOT module-based architecture
- NOT category-based organization

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:

1. ARCHITECTURE
   - Every layer operates independently
   - Late binding (runtime resolution)
   - No compile-time dependencies between layers
   - Support adding/removing layers without breaking system

2. DOORS SYSTEM
   - 1000 doors in grid
   - Doors never open
   - Click to initiate conversation
   - Each door = one investor

3. MISSIONS/TASKS
   - Missions: on-platform activities in Unity
   - Tasks: off-platform real work
   - Players opt in (never assigned)
   - Binary completion tracking

4. DIRECTORS
   - Real people (investors)
   - Do NOTHING - just there
   - System runs itself
   - No management, no review, no control

5. PLAYER FLOW
   - Approach door
   - Click door
   - See available missions
   - Opt in to task
   - Complete off-platform
   - Return for verification
   - Receive rewards

6. SCALE REQUIREMENTS
   - 10 million players minimum
   - 1000 investors maximum
   - Asynchronous interaction
   - Browser-based access

7. RESOURCES (EROS/SALT/VOID/FAVORS/KNOTS)
   - EROS: 0-100, life force/passion
   - SALT: 0-50, purification/authority
   - VOID: 0-unlimited, corruption (>100 = NPC)
   - FAVORS: social debts (>20 attracts Debt Collectors)
   - KNOTS: relationship bonds, visible commitments

8. RESOURCE CONVERSIONS
   - EROS → FAVORS (1:3) requires shared KNOT
   - SALT → EROS (2:1) requires moon alignment, +5 VOID
   - FAVORS → SALT (3:1) via truth ritual, lose unpaid favors
   - VOID → EROS (1:2) via pain ritual, permanent SALT -5
   - KNOT → VOID (strength:1) on betrayal, spawns 2 Debt Collectors

9. DATABASE SCHEMA
   directors: id, folklore_character, real_investor_id, agent_config
   players: id, eros, salt, void_score, favors, knots, tasks_completed
   tasks: id, director_id, type, requirements, duration_days, verification_method
   doors: director_id, branches, affinity_scores, daily_updates
   knots: id, player_a, player_b, strength, type, formed_date, visible
   branches: door_id, story_text, choices, eros_cost, salt_cost, void_effect
