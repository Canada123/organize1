## TEMPLATE USAGE GUIDELINES

### For Simple Questions:
- Use only: Executive Summary
- Skip: Most of the detailed analysis
- Time: 30 seconds

### For Moderate Questions:
- Use: Request Analysis (brief) + Primary Reasoning + Executive Summary
- Skip: Multiple perspectives (unless helpful)
- Time: 2-3 minutes

### For Complex Questions:
- Use: Full template except Appendix
- Include: 2-3 perspectives
- Time: 5-10 minutes

### For Expert Questions:
- Use: Complete template including Appendix
- Include: 4-5 perspectives
- Time: 10-20+ minutes
